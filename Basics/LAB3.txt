Create a weekly list containing the 7 days of the week.

1.From this list, how do you recover only the first 5 days of the
week on the one hand, and those of the weekend on the other hand? Use the subscript for this.
2.Find another way to achieve the same result (using another hint).
3.Find two ways to access the last day of the week.
4.Reverse the days of the week in one command.
